About Mbuyoti Kudzwa

We believe authentic African cuisine connects us to our roots and our communities. Mbuyoti Kudzwa (Ubuntu Kitchen) was founded to bring the rich, time-honoured recipes of Southern Africa to your table - the same meals shared at family gatherings, communal meals, and celebrations across the SADC region.

Every dish tells a story from Zimbabwe, Zambia, Botswana, South Africa, Mozambique and beyond. We use locally-sourced ingredients and traditional cooking methods passed down through generations. Our mission: to celebrate the spirit of ubuntu - the belief that we are because we are together - through every bite.
